## Data Quality Notes

- Each month tab lists staff in two side-by-side blocks: FEMALE STAFF and MALE STAFF, each with its own Sr. No./Name/Basic Pay/Net Payable/Signature columns.
- 'saved' = (Grand Total Basic Pay) - (Grand Total Net Payable) for that month, i.e. total amount withheld/unpaid across all staff. Verified consistent with the sheet's own Grand Total figures for all 6 months.
- The first three male entries each month (PRO. AZAM SIDDIQUE, HAFIZ FAIZAN AZAM, HASSAN AZAM) have no Basic Pay/Net Payable recorded in any month - they are listed but not part of this cash salary register.
- March and April sheets contain a 'consolidated_register': a secondary table further down the sheet that re-lists the same female+male staff as one sequential list. Verified the totals match the primary tables exactly. May and June also have this secondary table.
- January and February sheets do NOT have a consolidated_register section.
- June's consolidated_register has one extra column, headed literally '----' in the source file (meaning not specified) with a per-person value (mostly 850 or 0), totaling 11050.
- TOTAL STAFF MEMBERS declared in-sheet does not match the actual row count for March (declared 25, actual 27 rows) and April (declared 25, actual 28 rows). Likely not updated after staff were added mid-month. All other months match.
- JULY through DECEMBER tabs exist in the workbook but contain no data (template only).
